Job Information

Retail and Sales is a major industry and keeps our capitalist society running. This category covers a wide range of jobs varying from advertising to customer service and everything inbetween, it is a major, highly grossing category that rakes in millions each year. There are hundreds of thousands of jobs one can pick in this sector. These jobs are usually low to mid paid, unless you are high up or specialised, but these low to mid paid job places do not usually require a degree, however sometimes, experience is required. It is usually a sector that you can advance in easily, you must have good customer service and social skills in order to be effective in this sector. Advertising is a huge part of sales and extremely important in maintaining a brand, therefore people in the sales sector that specialise in advertising are poised to make good income. Customer service assistants and managers have the role of assisting the needs of customers and helping them when buying or viewing their businesses product. They help their business by making the customer happy, allowing for more customers via good recommendations and word of mouth.
